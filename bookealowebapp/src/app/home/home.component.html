Welcome to Bookealo

Bookealo is an intuitive platform designed for small businesses to easily set up and manage their public booking systems.

  • 📅 Manage schedules and availability with ease
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Assign users and roles to assets
  • 🔒 Handle bookings and blockings effortlessly
  • ⚙️ Customizable for various business types: tennis courts, barber seats, vehicles, and more

Whether you're running a salon, sports venue, or rental service, Bookealo gives you the control you need without the overhead.
